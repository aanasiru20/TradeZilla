💡 WHY THIS APPROACH

  Traditional React SPA:
    ❌ Need separate backend (Express, Python, etc.)
    ❌ Complex deployment (frontend + backend)
    ❌ Manual auth setup
    ❌ Harder to scale for multi-user
    ❌ Slower time to market
    
  TradeZilla with Next.js:
    ✓ Full-stack in one repo
    ✓ Simple deployment to Vercel
    ✓ Supabase Auth built-in
    ✓ Multi-user from day 1
    ✓ MVP in 2-3 weeks
    ✓ Scales to thousands of users
